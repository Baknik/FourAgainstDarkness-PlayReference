Dispel all illusion effects on anything within the current [[Tile]], including the party, enemies, and objects.

Enemies which are [[Invisible]] become visible and lose any advantages invisibility gave them. If no advantages were listed, subtract `2` from their `L`.

During [[Encounter]]s that act as events (like [[Invisible Gremlins]]), you may cast Disbelief as a free action at the beginnning of the [[Encounter]].
