Creates a mist around the party that:
- Stops all [[Ranged]] and Gaze [[Attack]]s in this [[Tile]], including the party's
- Makes [[Search]]ing impossible
- Provides the party with a `+2` bonus to [[Defense]] rolls while [[Flee|Fleeing]]

You may cast this as a free action in response to a [[Ranged]] or [[Gaze]] [[Attack]], or just before [[Flee|Fleeing]].

The mist lasts until you leave the [[Tile]] or [[Death|Die]].
