Summon a servant with `Tier` life. The party may only have one illusionary servant at a time.

The servant can carry `1` [[Armor]], `3` [[Weapon]]s, `1` [[Shield]], and `200`gp. It has 2 life and cannot perform any actions. Area attacks will damage it.

The servant lasts until either it is reduced to `0` life or you decide to dispel it.
