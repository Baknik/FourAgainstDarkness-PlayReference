Summon a servant with [[Tier]] life. The party may only have one illusionary servant at a time.

While the servant is present, carrying too many items cannot make you [[Overencumbered]]. This only applies to you, not the entire party.

The servant takes a place in the [[Marching Order]] and gains +2 to [[Defense]] rolls, but it cannot perform any actions.

The servant lasts until either it is reduced to 0 life or you decide to dispel it.
