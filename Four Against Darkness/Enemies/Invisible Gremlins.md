Invisible Gremlins work as an event rather than an enemy. As long as they are [[Invisible]] and cannot be seen by the party, they steal `d6+3` items in the following priority:
1. [[Magical]] items
2. [[Scroll]]s
3. [[Potion]]s
4. [[Weapon]]s
5. [[Gem]]s
6. Gold (10 gp per theft)

If the gremlins steal all of the party's items, they are left with a "Thank You" note that provides `1` [[Clue]].

If the gremlins can be seen or are no longer [[Invisible]], treat them as the following enemy:

| **Number** |    **`L`**    |  **Type**  | **Treasure** |
| :--------: | :-----------: | :--------: | :----------: |
|  `d6 + 1`  | `1/2 HCL + 3` | [[Minion]] |   `1` roll   |
Whenever a [[Character]] fails a [[Defense]] roll against them, they steal one item based on the priority list above.
