Invisible Gremlins work as an event rather than an enemy. They are [[Invisible]], and if the party cannot see them, they steal `d6+3` items in the following priority:
1. [[Magical]] items
2. [[Scroll]]s
3. [[Potion]]s
4. [[Weapon]]s
5. [[Gem]]s
6. Gold (10 gp per theft)

If the gremlins steal all of the party's items, they leave a "Thank You" note that provides `1` [[Clue]].

If the gremlins can be seen or are no longer [[Invisible]], treat them as the following enemy:

| **Number** |    **`L`**    |  **Type**  | **Damage** | **Treasure** |
| :--------: | :-----------: | :--------: | :--------: | :----------: |
|  `d6 + 1`  | `1/2 HCL + 3` | [[Minion]] |     0      |   `1` roll   |
Whenever a [[Character]] fails a [[Defense]] roll against them, instead of dealing damage, they steal one item from that [[Character]] based on the priority list above.
