[[Elf]] and elf-type classes [[Attack]] Orcs at `+1`.

At the start of their turn, Orcs must roll [[Morale]] if at least `1` of them was defeated by a [[Spell]] in the previous player turn.

If a [[Spell]] is what reduces them to lower than half their numbers, they roll [[Morale]] at `-1`.

They never have [[Magical]] items in their treasure, If you roll one, you find `d6 x d6`gp instead.
