Each enemy turn, each Troll that has been killed has a `2-in-6` chance to regenerate (come back to life via natural healing). This can be prevented by slaying them with a [[Spell]], [[Fire]], or [[Acid]] attack. A [[Character]] can also spend their action to hack the Trolls' bodies to pieces with a slashing melee [[Weapon]].
