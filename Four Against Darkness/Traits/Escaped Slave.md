You recently escaped enslavement, and your former masters are on your trail.
Roll a `d3` on the below table to determine who your former masters were.

| `1` | [[Kobold Slavemasters]] |
| --- | ----------------------- |
| `2` | [[Chaos Dwarves]]       |
| `3` | [[Wood Elves]]          |
Each time the party encounters a [[Minion]] [[Wandering Enemy]], there is a `3-in-6` chance they are replaced by a group of your former masters. Once `30` of your former masters are killed, they will stop pursuing and you gain `1` XP.
