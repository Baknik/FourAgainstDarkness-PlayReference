You recently escaped enslavement, and your former masters are on your trail.
Roll a `d3` on the below table to determine who your former masters were.

| `1-2` | [[Kobold Slavemasters]] |
| ----- | ----------------------- |
| `3-4` | [[Chaos Dwarves]]       |
| `5-6` | [[Wood Elves]]          |
Each time the party encounters a [[Minion]] [[Wandering Enemy]], there is a `1-in-3` chance they are replaced by your masters.
Once `30` of your masters are killed, they will stop searching and you gain `1` XP.
