Once per [[Long Rest]], after casting an #illusion  [[Spell]], you may become [[Invisible]] and can choose to:
- [[Fleeing|Flee]] back to the previous [[Tile]] without being attacked
- Remain in the current [[Tile]] and perform one free [[Melee]] [[Attack]] during the next enemy turn.
After performing the chosen action, you are no longer [[Invisible]].

#trait #illusionist
