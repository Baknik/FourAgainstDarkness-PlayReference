Any [[Character]] in position `1` or `2` of the [[Marching Order]] may attempt to disarm a [[Detect Trap|Detected Trap]] by succeeding a [[Disarm]] [[Save]] against the trap's `L`. If the disarm fails, the trap triggers. The [[Character]] who attempted the disarm is always included as one of the trap's targets.

| Class     | Bonus |
| --------- | ----- |
| [[Rogue]] | +L    |
