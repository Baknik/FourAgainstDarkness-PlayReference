This item has `1` charge.

When you fail a [[Defense]] roll, you may use `1` charge to instead pass that roll, teleporting you to safety. You may not return to the party until the end of the [[Encounter]].

When it doesn't have any charges remaining, this item is only worth `d6 + 1`gp.
