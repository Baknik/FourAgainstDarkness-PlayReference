##### Between Adventure and Settlement

Roll `Tier d3` to determine the travel distance between the [[Settlement]] and the [[Adventure]]. 

Each trip (there and back again), a chosen [[Character]] can attempt as many [[Orientation]] [[Save]]s against the [[Adventure]] [[Biome]]'s [[Orientation]] difficulty as desired until failing. Each success reduces the travel distance for that trip by `1` (to a minimum of `Tier`), but each attempt after the first increases the difficulty by `1`.

To make the journey in either direction, you must spend a number of [[Ration]]s per [[Character]] equal to the travel distance.

A chosen [[Character]] can attempt as many [[Forage]] [[Save]]s against the [[Adventure]] [[Biome]]'s [[Forage]] difficulty as desired until failing. Each success grants `1` [[Ration]] per [[Character]], but each attempt after the first increases the difficulty by `1`.

For every [[Ration]] you do not have, one [[Character]] loses `1` life.

After [[Ration]]s have been spent, there is a `1-in-6` chance of having a [[Wilderness Encounter]].
##### Between Settlements

This travel is done along civilized roads, so no [[Ration]]s are needed, and travel distance is ignored.

There is a `1-in-6` chance of having a [[Road Encounter]].
