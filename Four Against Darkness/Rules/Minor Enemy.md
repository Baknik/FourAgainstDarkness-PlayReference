[[Vermin]] and [[Minion]] enemies are designated as minor enemies. 

Minor enemies have `1` life each and are encountered in groups. Each damage dealt to a minor enemy group kills `1` of them.

They divide their [[Attack]]s evenly among all [[Party Member]]s involved in the [[Combat]].

Once per [[Encounter]], when the number of minor enemies in a group is reduced to below half of their initial number, they make a [[Morale]] roll.

Defeating `10` groups of minor enemies grants `1` XP.
