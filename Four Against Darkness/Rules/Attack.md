Roll the [[Party Member]]'s die and add any applicable bonuses. If the total is >= the enemy's `L`, the target is [[Hit]].

Multiple [[Hit]]s can be achieved by beating the enemy's `L` multiple times with the same roll.

*For example, a roll total of `22` vs an enemy `L` of `10` will [[Hit]] that enemy twice (the extra `2` is lost).*
