[[Weird]], [[Boss]], and [[Final Boss]] enemies are designated as major enemies. 

Major enemies have a large life pool. Each damage dealt to a major enemy removes `1` life.

They often have multiple [[Attack]]s with different rules for which [[Party Member]]s to target.

Once per [[Encounter]], when a major enemy's life is reduced to below half of its initial number, they make a [[Morale]] roll. If they succeed, reduce their `L` by `1` until the end of the [[Encounter]].

Defeating a major enemy grants `1` XP.
