To defeat an enemy non-lethally (*usually for the purposes of taking them alive*), all [[Character]]s in the party must take a `-1` penalty to all of their [[Attack]] rolls for the entire [[Encounter]].

Once the enemy is brought to `0` life in this way, they must be [[Bound]], either with a [[Rope]] or some other method.

[[Spell]]s that deal damage **cannot** be used to subdue, but [[Sleep]] will subdue instantly.

[[Vermin]], [[Horde]]s, and #unliving enemies **cannot** be subdued.

#rule
