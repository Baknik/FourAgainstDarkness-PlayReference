When the [[Party]] approaches a door, roll on the following table.
A door's `L` is `HCL + d6`.

A destroyed door may not be nailed shut with a [[Bag of Nails]].

| `2d6` | Door Type                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| :---: | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|   2   | Magically sealed. Cannot be bashed. The party has a single attempt to open it with a successful [[Spell]] [[Attack]] vs the door's `L`. On a result of `1`, either the caster takes `2` damage, or a [[Wandering Enemy]] arrives. Add `+1` to any treasure roll made in the [[Tile]] beyond this door.                                       |
|   3   | Iron door that is locked. Cannot be bashed. Each [[Character]] who can lockpick may attempt one [[Lockpicking]] [[Save]]. Successful lockpicking makes the door Unlocked. The door can also be destroyed by a [[Fireball]] or [[Lightning]] [[Spell]] (no roll needed). Add `+1` to any treasure roll made in the [[Tile]] beyond this door. |
|   4   | Illusionary door. Can only be opened by spending `3` [[Clue]]s, or with a successful [[Spell]] [[Attack]] by an [[Illusionist]] vs `HCL` (only one attempt per [[Illusionist]]).                                                                                                                                                             |
|  5-6  | Locked. Can be Unlocked with a successful [[Bash]] [[Save]] vs the door's `L`. Can also be Unlocked with a successful [[Lockpicking]] [[Save]] vs the door's `L`. The door can also be destroyed by a [[Fireball]] or [[Lightning]] [[Spell]] (no roll needed).                                                                              |
| 7-10  | Unlocked. Can be freely opened and closed.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                   |
|  11   | Trapped. This door is unlocked, but is protected by a `HCL + d6` `L` trap that triggers when the door is opened, targeting the [[Character]] in position `1` of the [[Marching Order]]. The target must succeed a [[Trap]] [[Save]] or take `1` damage.                                                                                      |
|  12   | Lever door. Can only be opened by spending `1` [[Clue]] or `1` [[Gadget Point]]. If the party [[Withdraw]]s through this door, enemies cannot attack them as they leave.                                                                                                                                                                     |
