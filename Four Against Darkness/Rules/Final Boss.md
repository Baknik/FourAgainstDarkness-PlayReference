A final boss enemy is considered a [[Major Enemy]] and are either specific boss enemies with their own rules, or buffed regular boss enemies.

Whenever the party encounters a [[Boss]], roll `d6` and add the total number of [[Major Enemy|Major Enemies]] already encountered this [[Rules/Adventure]]. If the result is `>= 6`, the final boss is encountered.

If the final boss is a regular boss, use the `HCL` scaling below for its life (instead of `Tier`), add `1` extra attack, and add `1` [[Weird Treasure]] to its treasure.

Their life is `HCL` plus a constant modifier.
Their `L` is `HCL` plus a constant modifier.
At least one of their attacks deals `Tier` damage.
