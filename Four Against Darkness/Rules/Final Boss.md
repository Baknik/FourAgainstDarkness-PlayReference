A final boss enemy is considered a [[Major Enemy]] and are either specific boss enemies with their own rules, or buffed regular boss enemies.

Whenever the party encounters a [[Boss]], roll `d6` and add the total number of [[Major Enemy|Major Enemies]] already encountered this [[Adventure]]. If the result is `>= 6`, the final boss is encountered.

If the final boss is a regular boss, use the `HCL` scaling below for its life (instead of [[Tier]]), add `1` extra attack, and double it's treasure.

Their life is `HCL` plus a constant modifier.

Their `L` is `HCL` plus a constant modifier.
