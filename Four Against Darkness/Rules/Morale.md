When making a morale roll, roll a `d6` for the subject.
- On a `1-3`, morale fails and the subject flees. If the morale roll was caused by the party and an enemy flees, that enemy is still considered defeated and contributes to XP.
- On a `4-6` morale succeeds and the subject stays.

Enemies with the [[Reaction]] "Fight to the Death" never roll morale.
