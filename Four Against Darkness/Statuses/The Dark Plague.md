When you are **exposed** to the plague, succeed a [[Resist Disease]] [[Save]] vs `10` or catch the Dark Plague ([[Halfling]]s add `+1/2 L`).

Each time an infected [[Character]] enters a new [[Tile]] or [[Hex]], roll their [[Character Die]]. On a `1`, they lose `1` [[Life]]. Regardless of that result, each other non-infected [[Character]] in the party is then **exposed** to the plague.

Can be cured with a [[Blessing]] [[Spell]] only if the caster succeeds a [[Healing]] [[Save]] vs `10`.
Eating [[Elven Bread]] cures the plague automatically.
A [[Character]] is immune to the plague until next [[Rest]] if they were cured or they resisted a [[Save]] to catch the plague.
