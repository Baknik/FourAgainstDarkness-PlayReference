When learning this [[Skill]], choose a specific [[Biome]] that you have adventured in. You use a higher [[Tier]] die when making an [[Attack]], [[Defense]], or [[Save]] roll in that [[Biome]] (ex. if you are in basic [[Tier]], you use the expert die). This effect is **cumulative** with [[Sworn Enemy]].

When your party successfully makes a [[Forage]] [[Save]] in the chosen [[Biome]], they find `1` additional [[Ration]].

This [[Skill]] may be learned multiple times.
