When learning this [[Skill]], choose a specific type of enemy (ex. Orc). You use a higher [[Tier]] die when making an [[Attack]], [[Defense]], or [[Stealth]] [[Save]] roll against that type of enemy (ex. if you are in Basic [[Tier]], you use the Expert die). This effect is **cumulative** with [[Learning From The Environment]].

This [[Skill]] may be learned multiple times.
