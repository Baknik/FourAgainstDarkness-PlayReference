The party is drenched after [[Travel]].

The party may not use [[Firearm]]s, and attacks from [[Ranged Weapon]]s and [[Light Ranged Weapon]]s lose `-1` on their [[Attack]] rolls.

This effect can be removed with a [[Short Rest]] if the entire party takes their rest action to light a fire and dry off their gear. After this, make `3` [[Wandering Enemy]] rolls (instead of `1`) and fight any that arrive independently.
